Kickstarter

Kickstarter's main goal is to relay the most necessary information at the top of each project page. Every project includes a video describing the creator's goals, obstacles, and any other information they feel is relevant when trying to sell their idea. If the video is enough to pique interest then the rest of the page contains more detailed information about the project. Every project page has the same information contained in the top right, which gives viewers a succinct but helpful look at how the project is performing.
