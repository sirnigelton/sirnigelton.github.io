The Basics of Disc Golf

Choosing the correct disc
There are many different varieties of discs out there, and if you're new to the sport then the number of choices can be overwhelming. For beginners, discs of a ligher weight tend to be easier to control, since they generally fly straight and glide well.
Heavier discs, on the other hand, require a higher degree of control when thrown because the higher weights make them more difficult to throw level, but their weight allows them to acheive greater distances.

Developing proper technique
The most important aspect of the throw to remember is always follow through on your shots.
